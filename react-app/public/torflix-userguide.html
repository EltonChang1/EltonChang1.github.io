Torflix — User Guide

A movie library on your hardware, aimed at a century of film in the wild, not this month's licensed row. Below: how to use the app—torflixd, home, rows, downloads, watch, and dashboard.

1. First time you open the app

You may see a short welcome / legal dialog. It reminds you that Torflix runs on your computer, that you should only access content you have the right to use, and where to find settings later.

Action: Click Got it to dismiss. You can use the app normally afterward.

2. Home — top bar and hero

Home opens with a large featured area and catalog rows below. In the top bar:

  • Torflix (logo) — return to Home.
  • Home — curated rows (trending, genres, recommendations, Dashboard rules).
  • Search — keyword search (when catalog search is enabled).
  • My downloads — torrents you added and their status.
  • Dashboard — customize Home; optional Sign in.

On the right: Quick search — type at least two characters and press Enter to jump to Search with that query.

3. Catalog rows

Below the hero, titles are grouped into horizontal rows. Scroll sideways to see more posters. Row labels depend on the catalog and your Dashboard (e.g. Recently added, Top 10, trending).

Click any poster to open title details (next section).

4. Title details — list, download, watch

After you click a poster, a detail panel opens with artwork, size, seeders, and actions:

  • Add to My List (heart) — saves the title in this browser; with an account, settings can sync on this server.
  • Full download — standard BitTorrent download (rarest-first). Best when you want to keep the file.
  • Watch while downloading — requests sequential download from the start so the Watch page can play while data arrives. Best for MP4/WebM; other formats may not play in the browser.
  • View source page — opens the listing site in a new tab (if provided).

If several torrents exist (different qualities), open Quality / version, pick a line, then Full download or Watch while downloading.

Legal: Only add and share content you are permitted to use.

5. Search

Use Search in the top bar or Quick search. Results open the same title details flow as Home.

6. My downloads

Lists everything you added: progress, status, and shortcuts to Watch for jobs started with Watch while downloading.

7. Watching in the browser

Playback opens on the Watch page. If the stream stalls, wait for more data and press Play again. Volume may be remembered on this device.

Typical shortcuts (see on-page hints): Space play/pause, arrows seek, M mute, F fullscreen.

8. Dashboard — customize Home

  • Favorite genres — comma-separated; feeds recommendation-style rows.
  • Toggle recommendation rows on or off.
  • Show/hide Home rows and reorder with up/down controls.
  • Save — local for guests; on the server if you register and sign in.

Sign-in is optional; it mainly syncs Dashboard preferences and continue watching on this server.

9. Connection & BitTorrent

At the bottom of the page, expand Connection & BitTorrent to see WebSocket status and the listener summary. Slow downloads or missing peers may involve firewall, VPN, or port settings on the machine running torflixd.

10. Troubleshooting

Issue What to try
“Catalog unavailable” Daemon needs the embedded catalog API or a configured search API. You can still use My downloads.
Video won’t play Format may be unsupported in-browser; use Full download and a local player.
Slow / no peers Swarms and trackers vary; check firewall/VPN and listener status.
Search missing Catalog search may be disabled on the server.

Install and configuration: see the README and Configuration on GitHub.
